TEAMWORK PERSONIFIED

Privately owned and based from its headquarters in Courtenay, BC, AFC Construction has been providing project management, construction management and general contracting services on a wide variety of projects since the founding of the company in 2004. Partners Guthrie LeFevre and Alan Fletcher bring extensive knowledge and complimentary skills to bear on every job site, and with each client. Both are actively involved in the day-to-day operations of the company, and take immense pride in the collaborative, team-based environment they create on every project. Our dedication to quality begins with our commitment to employees. Carpenters, subcontractors and employees that are proud of their work, and bring years of experience to every job site.
